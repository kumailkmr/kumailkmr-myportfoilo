# Workspace Rules for Kumail KMR's Portfolio

- **Identity**: The portfolio belongs to "Kumail KMR", a premium independent AI & Software Engineer.
- **Design Philosophy**: The site must maintain an ultra-premium, high-end visual aesthetic on par with Linear and Vercel. Always prioritize deep glassmorphism (backdrop blurs), subtle inner shadows, cinematic background ambient glows, and highly refined 1px borders over flat colors.
- **Copywriting Tone**: Copy must always be tailored toward "client closing" for a premium freelancer. Avoid sounding like a faceless agency. Emphasize "Direct Developer Access", "No Agency Overhead", and high-end software solutions. 
- **Animations**: Prefer subtle, smooth spring-based micro-animations (via Framer Motion) and interactive hover effects (like the `hover-lift` utility and glowing button states).
